How it works

This questionnaire uses the Climatiq API to calculate an estimate of your carbon footprint based on emission factors (how quickly greenhouse gases (GHGs) are released into the atmosphere) from 5 key areas of your life.

  1. Your household
  2. Your electricity usage and the fuels (renewable vs fossil) your state uses to generate electricity
  3. Your modes of transport for moving around the world
  4. And lastly, your diet and shopping habits

We've chosen the Climatiq API for the most accurate and industry standard estimations. You can find out more about these calculations in how it works.

Once you've complete the quiz hit submit and we'll show you to the results page and breakdown your score and how you're doing compared to the current average.

We will provide you with quick and easy options to reduce your carbon footprint that will also increase your savings, improve your life and make you proud.
