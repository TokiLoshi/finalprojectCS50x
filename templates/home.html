This carbon footprint tracker turns you into a hero


Not all heroes wear capes, but we see the hero in you. Also, there's a leaderboard waiting full of heroes waiting to welcome you.


We help you turn small green choices into major wins!

Calculate your carbon footprint and get your results on the app in seconds.

Carbon Hero aims to make it fun, easy and engaging to make greener decisions that reduce your carbon footprint, save you money and build a more resilient community along with your peers.

Accept challenges and compete with your community for points. Choose your leaderboardname, or use our random leaderboard generator to help you pick one or give you inspration. Everything you do on the app earns you points and we'll be there rooting for you.

Changing out one bulb? Going meatless for one day? No green action, no matter how small you may think it, goes unappreciated. We'll be giving you kudos the whole way.

Nothing here is set in stone. Your carbon footprint when you start is just the beginning of a world of improvement. With all this potential for change, we understand you want to change your appearance from time to time. Feel free to customize your icons and leaderboardname as often as you feel like.
