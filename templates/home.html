This carbon footprint tracker turns you into a hero


We can only fight the worst impacts of climate change if we work together. Scientists keep issuing grim warnings about our future, given our current trajectory. First, we looked to policymakers to guide us, but bureaucracy moves slowly. Then we looked to corporate America, but the status quo profit margins were too comfortable for them to make significant changes. Right now, we need a superhero to come and save us. We see that hero in you. Carbon Hero makes it easy and fun to make greener choices and rewards you for every green choice. A community of heroes is waiting to welcome you to the leaderboard. Let's do this.


We help you turn small green choices into major wins!

Our carbon footprint calculator, powered by Climatiq's API, will estimate your current carbon footprint and provide you with a detailed breakdown in seconds.

Carbon Hero aims to make it fun, easy, and engaging to make greener decisions that reduce your carbon footprint, save you money and help you build a more resilient community. Nervous about our future? We were too, until we saw dedicated carbon heroes like you.

Up for a challenge? You make a pledge based on how you want to challenge yourself. Each challenge you accept and complete in the app earns you points. Log a completed challenge in the app and watch your points and savings accumulate, with instant updates on the leaderboard.

Unlike fossil fuels, your footprint is not set in stone. We use your initial carbon footprint calculation as a benchmark for your future choices and will never pressure you into purchasing offsets. We help you visualize how much carbon your greener decisions are saving you and how much money they save you.

Whether you pledge to change out one incandescent bulb for an LED bulb or go meatless for one day of the week, you are committing to positively impacting your life, savings, and community. No action is too small, and no effort goes unappreciated or unrewarded. We'll be dealing out kudos and cheering you on every step of the way. Have some fun with your leaderboard name and icons, these are also not set in stone, and you can change them whenever you like.
